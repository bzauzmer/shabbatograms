About Us


Rabbi Shayna Golkow spent thirteen summers at Camp Ramah in the Poconos as a camper, counselor, Rosh Edah (Division Head), and Scholar in Residence. She was ordained as a rabbi in 2019 and currently serves as the Second Rabbi at Temple Aliyah in Woodland Hills, CA. Her favorite camp tradition is Kabbalat Shabbat overlooking the lake.
